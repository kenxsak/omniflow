## Executive Summary

OmniFlow is a white-label, India-first CRM and marketing automation platform competing against both Indian players (LeadSquared, Kylas, Freshsales, Privyr, AiSensy/WATI) and global giants (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Zoho, ActiveCampaign, Vendasta).

### Key Differentiators
1. **White-Label Ready** - Full rebrand capability for agencies
2. **BYOK Model** - Bring Your Own Keys for AI/messaging (no markup)
3. **India-First** - IndiaMART, JustDial, GST invoicing, DLT compliance
4. **All-in-One** - CRM + Marketing + AI + Voice in single subscription
5. **Affordable** - Fraction of competitor pricing
